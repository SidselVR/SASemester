There are two versions of this game, one for the pc, and one for Virtual reality! Do mind, that the VR version only works on android phones.

PC controls:

To look around hold down the left mousebutton and drag your mouse around. Two boxes will appear when you're asked a question where you simply click one to answer. You can drink by clicking on any bottle and/or bevarage.

VR controls:

As this is a virtual reality game, simply move your phone around to look around. When people ask you questions you answer them by nodding or shaking your head. You can drink by looking at any bottle and/or bevarage for 1.5 seconds.
